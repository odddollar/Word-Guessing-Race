How To Play

Aim: Guess all 10 words correctly before the other players

  1. On the home page enter a username and press ENTER or click "Go!"
  2. A 5 letter word with the 2nd and 4th letters missing will appear
  3. Enter your guess for the entire word (case sensitive) in the box
  4. The box will turn green when the guessed word is correct
  5. Press ENTER or click "Submit Guess" to move to the next word
  6. When 1 player guesses all 10 words the game stops

Note: All words are nouns, verbs or adjectives with no names. It's possible for a word to occur more than once

Note: As soon as a someone submits a username they're able to place guesses. When playing in a group, ensure everyone submits at the same time
